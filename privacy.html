Majestic Labs Inc. — Employee Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 1, 2023

Last Updated: October 15, 2025

This Employee Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how Majestic Labs Inc. (“Majestic Labs,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information about our employees, contractors, job applicants, and related personnel.

Majestic Labs is a privately held company based in California, USA, with operations in Tel Aviv, Israel. We are focused on the design and manufacture of AI hardware and server systems. This Policy applies to all personal information processed in the context of employment, recruitment, and internal operations.

Majestic Labs does not currently provide products or services to consumers and does not process customer or end-user data.

1. Scope

This Policy applies to:

● Current and former employees of Majestic Labs;

● Job applicants and candidates;

● Contractors, consultants, and interns;

● Individuals whose information is provided to us in connection with employment (e.g., emergency contacts, dependents, beneficiaries).

2. Categories of Personal Information We Collect

Depending on your role and relationship with Majestic Labs, we may collect and process the following categories of personal information:

a. Identification and Contact Information

● Name, address, phone number, and email address;

● Date of birth, government-issued identifiers (such as Social Security number, national ID, or tax ID);

● Emergency contact information.

b. Employment and Professional Information

● Employment history, qualifications, résumé/CV, and references;

● Position, department, and reporting line;

● Compensation and benefits information;

● Performance evaluations, disciplinary records, and training data;

● Work-related communications and correspondence.

c. Financial and Payroll Information

● Bank account or payment information (for payroll);

● Tax forms and withholding details;

● Equity or stock option data (if applicable).

d. IT and Security Information

● Use of company devices, systems, and applications;

● Security logs, access credentials, and badge records;

● Video surveillance (if applicable at office sites).

e. Sensitive or Special Categories (where permitted by law)

● Health or medical information (e.g., sick leave or accommodation requests); ● Immigration or visa documentation;

● Demographic information voluntarily provided for diversity, equity, and inclusion programs.


3. How We Use Personal Information

We process personal information for legitimate business and employment-related purposes, including:

● Recruiting, hiring, and onboarding personnel;

● Managing employment relationships and HR administration;

● Administering payroll, benefits, and tax obligations;

● Ensuring workplace safety, security, and compliance;

● Maintaining company systems, devices, and networks;

● Meeting legal and regulatory requirements;

● Internal reporting, planning, and business operations.

We do not sell, rent, or share employee data for marketing or advertising purposes.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (Where Applicable)

Depending on your jurisdiction, we process personal data based on:

● Performance of an employment contract;

● Compliance with legal obligations (e.g., labor, tax, or immigration law); ● Legitimate business interests, such as maintaining security or efficient operations; ● Consent, where specifically required by law.

5. Data Sharing and Transfers

We may share personal information with:

● Payroll, benefits, and HR service providers;

● IT and cybersecurity vendors;

● Accounting, legal, and compliance professionals;

● Government agencies or regulators (where required by law).

Because Majestic Labs operates in both the United States and Israel, personal information may be transferred between these jurisdictions. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and safeguard measures.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for employment, legal, and operational purposes or as required by law. When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Data Security

Majestic Labs employs reasonable physical, technical, and administrative safeguards to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Access to personal data is restricted to individuals who require it for legitimate business purposes.

8. Employee Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

● Access or request a copy of your personal data;

● Request correction or deletion of inaccurate data;

● Object to or restrict processing under certain circumstances;

● Withdraw consent (where processing is based on consent);

● File a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact hr-us@majestic-labs.ai. Verification of identity may be required before fulfilling certain requests.

9. Monitoring and Company Systems

Majestic Labs may monitor the use of company systems, networks, and devices to maintain security, ensure policy compliance, and protect company assets.

Such monitoring is limited to legitimate business purposes and conducted in compliance with applicable laws.

10. Minors

Majestic Labs does not employ or engage individuals under the age of 18 and does not intentionally collect personal information from minors.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be communicated internally and posted on company systems with a revised “Last Updated” date.

Annex: California Privacy Notice for Employees (CCPA/CPRA Compliance)

This Annex applies to California residents who are current or former employees, job applicants, contractors, or similar personnel of Majestic Labs Inc., in compliance with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA).

A. Categories of Personal Information Collected

We collect the following categories of personal information, as defined by California law: 1. Identifiers — name, contact details, government ID numbers.

2. Employment-related information — position, compensation, performance data. 3. Financial information — bank account details, tax information, and payroll data.

4. Internet or electronic network activity — use of company devices, systems, or applications.

5. Sensitive personal information — health data, biometric identifiers (if any), immigration data.

6. Inferences — professional or work-related evaluations or assessments.

B. Purposes for Collection and Use

Majestic Labs uses personal information solely for:

● Managing employment and HR functions;

● Administering payroll, benefits, and equity programs;

● Ensuring workplace safety, security, and compliance;

● Legal, tax, and audit obligations;

● Recruiting and workforce management.

We do not sell or share employee personal information and do not use it for advertising or marketing purposes.

C. Retention

We retain employee and applicant information only as long as needed for business, legal, and compliance purposes, consistent with Section 6 of this Policy.

D. Your Rights under California Law

As a California resident, you may have the right to:

● Know what categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect about you;

● Request deletion of certain personal information (subject to legal exceptions);

● Correct inaccurate information;

● Limit use or disclosure of sensitive personal information;

● Be free from retaliation for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a request, contact hr-us@majestic-labs.ai.

We may verify your identity before processing requests.
